Glossary

A Business Application, or Application is a piece of software which a customer uses to perform at least one business capability within GSA. For more information, please see the presentation What is an Application?

A Parent System, or System is a concept that groups together similar and related Business Applications that work together to deliver enhanced business capabilities.

A Technology is intended to describe those component parts of Business Applications that work together to deliver a business capability.

The concept of the Platform is intended to describe a software development environment that the applications, systems, etc. are built upon.

An Informational Website is intended to represent a non-interactive presentation of information.

Cloud indicates the hosting location of the data. If the data are not on Cloud, the Hosting Provider is GSA. Otherwise, if the data are on cloud, the Hosting Provider is an external provider.

For more information on the relationship of key definitions, please refer to An Agile Approach to Federal Enterprise Architecture.


FISMA is the abbreviation for Federal Information Security Modernization Act, please visit here for additional information.
